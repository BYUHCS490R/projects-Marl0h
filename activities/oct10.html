Notes

Most tags are block tags. (h1, body, main, head, header, etc.)
List of inline tags: a (anchor link), span, img, em,strong, mark. ( learned about br tags recently)

Questions

  • What is the difference between block tags and inline tags?
    --A block tag takes an entire area, where an inline tag does a line inside a block without a line break. Block tags are made of boxes. Boxes/Blocks cannot share horizontal space
  • What is the margin, border, and padding?
    --The margin is the space outside of the box
    --The border is the width of the edge of the box
    --The padding is the space inside the box
  • What are units of measurement you can use in CSS?
    --The unit of measurement most commonly used is pixels, with other options like percentage, rem
  • When would you use a figure tag?
    -- A figure tag is a img tag that makes the image a block tag instead of a inline tag like the img tag is.
